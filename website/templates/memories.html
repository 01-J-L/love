Happy Valentine's Day

Valentine's Day 2024 was so funny! At first, you told me that you didn't want anything. I kept asking what you wanted, but you never said what it could be. When the package arrived, it turned out to be what I wanted, a LEGO flower set. You said you didn't know what to get me, so you just got something for yourself that I also happened to really want. Honestly, I was so happy. And I made you a coupon, but you never used it, so now I get to keep it for myself, haha. Next time, just tell me what you want, don't get it for me and say it's for you!

The funniest part was the next day when I sent you a LEGO flower set too! I told you I already knew what you wanted, because it was what I truly wanted. So, you ended up buying me the LEGO flowers, and I got a set for you. It was like we were on the same wavelength without even talking.

Thank you, Wissel. This was my favorite Valentine's Day ever!
